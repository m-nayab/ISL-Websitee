KERAL BLASTERS

Kerala Blasters Football Club is the most successful football club in Kerala. The club has reached the Indian Super League final three times, but has yet to win the championship. They finished as runners-up in the 2014, 2016, and 2022 seasons.

The Blasters are known for their attacking style of play and their passionate fan base. The club has a strong youth academy and is committed to developing Indian talent.

Manjappada

Yellow Army

The Manjappada is known for its vibrant atmosphere at home matches, where they create a sea of yellow and sing their hearts out for the team. They are also known for their passionate support on social media, where they are always vocal in their support of the club

Jersey

The Kerala Blasters jersey is a yellow base with a dark blue stripe running down the middle, symbolizing the club's identity and passionate fan base.
